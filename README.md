# 📊 Decathlon India Sales, Customer performace & Product Analytics Dashboard | Power BI Project

## 🚀 Project Overview

The **Decathlon India Sales, Customer & Product Analytics Dashboard** is a comprehensive Business Intelligence solution developed using **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze retail and e-commerce business performance. This project was created as part of an MCA Internship and demonstrates the complete implementation of the Business Intelligence lifecycle, from raw data processing to interactive dashboard development and advanced analytics reporting.

In today's competitive retail environment, organizations generate massive volumes of transactional and customer data. However, transforming this data into meaningful insights that support strategic decision-making remains a major challenge. This dashboard addresses that challenge by consolidating business data into a centralized, interactive, and visually appealing reporting platform.

The project focuses on providing management teams, business analysts, sales managers, and decision-makers with a clear understanding of business performance through dynamic visualizations, key performance indicators (KPIs), trend analysis, customer behavior insights, and product performance tracking.

The dashboard enables users to monitor sales growth, identify profitable products, understand customer purchasing patterns, evaluate regional performance, and make data-driven decisions that contribute to business growth and operational efficiency.
